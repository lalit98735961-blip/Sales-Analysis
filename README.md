# Sales-Analysis
This project is a Sales Analysis Dashboard built using Power BI to analyze the sales performance of a Superstore. The dashboard provides an interactive view of key business metrics such as total sales, profit, yearly growth, product performance, customer age groups, and monthly sales trends.

**Tools & Technologies
-Power BI
-Power Query
-DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
-Data Modeling
-Data Visualization
-Bookmarks
-Slicers
-KPIs

**Key Features
-Interactive Sales Dashboard
-KPI Cards for Total Sales, Profit, and Yearly Sales
-Monthly and Yearly Sales Trend Analysis
-Category & Sub-Category Performance Analysis
-Customer Age Group Analysis
-Top & Bottom Performing Products
-Dynamic Filters (Year, Category, Sub-Category)
-Bookmark-based Navigation for a smooth and interactive user experience

Overall Business Performance
* Generated ₹22.97 Lakhs in total sales with a ₹2.86 Lakhs total profit.
* Achieved consistent business growth over four years, indicating improved market performance.

Sales Trend Analysis
 Sales gradually increased throughout the year, with the highest revenue recorded in November and December.
 Lower sales were observed during the early months of the year, indicating seasonal demand patterns.
 The dashboard helps identify peak sales periods for better inventory and marketing planning.

Year-wise Performance
 Sales showed continuous improvement from 2016 to 2019.
 2019 contributed the highest share of revenue (31.92% of total sales), making it the best-performing year.

Category Performance
 Technology emerged as the highest revenue-generating category, contributing 55.55% of total sales.
 Office Supplies delivered stable and consistent performance.
 Furniture contributed the lowest share, highlighting opportunities for improvement.

Product Performance
 Phones, Chairs, Storage, and Copiers were among the top-performing sub-categories.
 Bookcases and Accessories generated comparatively lower sales, indicating the need for strategic pricing or promotional activities.

Customer Insights
  Customers aged 25–54 years contributed the highest revenue.
  Younger customers (Under 25) generated the lowest sales, suggesting potential opportunities for targeted marketing campaigns.

Profitability Analysis
 Technology products generated both strong sales and healthy profits.
 The dashboard enables comparison of sales, profit, and quantity across different product categories for informed business decisions.

Interactive Dashboard Features
 Dynamic filtering by Year, Category, Sub-Category, and Top N Products.
 Enables users to drill down into sales performance and identify trends across multiple business dimensions.

# Super Store Sales Analysis using SQL

## Project Overview

In this project, I analyzed the Super Store sales dataset using SQL Server to understand sales performance, customer behavior, product performance, regional trends, shipping, and the impact of discounts. The goal was to turn raw data into meaningful insights that can help businesses make better decisions.

## What I Did

* Imported the Super Store dataset into SQL Server and explored the data.
* Calculated the total number of orders, repeat customers, unique customers, total products, and total quantity sold.
* Analyzed total sales, total profit, average sales, average profit, highest sales, and lowest sales.
* Counted the number of product categories and sub-categories.
* Found the top 10 customers based on sales and profit, customers who placed the most orders, average spending per customer, and the most valuable customer.
* Compared sales and profit across different customer segments.
* Identified the best-selling, least-selling, most profitable, and least profitable products.
* Analyzed sales, profit, and quantity sold for each product category.
* Calculated the average discount for each product and found products that were sold at a loss.
* Compared sales and profit across different states and regions and identified loss-making states and states with the highest discounts.
* Analyzed monthly and yearly sales and profit trends, including the most and least profitable months.
* Evaluated shipping performance by comparing sales, profit, total orders, and average shipping time for each shipping mode.
* Studied how discounts affect sales and profit by analyzing discount levels, products with the highest discounts, and orders with discounts above 20%.
