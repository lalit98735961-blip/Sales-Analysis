# Sales-Analysis
This project is a Sales Analysis Dashboard built using Power BI to analyze the sales performance of a Superstore. The dashboard provides an interactive view of key business metrics such as total sales, profit, yearly growth, product performance, customer age groups, and monthly sales trends.

**Business Insights**

The Superstore Sales Dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of sales performance from 2016 to 2019, enabling stakeholders to monitor revenue trends, product performance, customer segments, and yearly growth.

** Overall Business Performance**
Generated ₹22.97 Lakhs in total sales with a ₹2.86 Lakhs total profit.
Achieved consistent business growth over four years, indicating improved market performance.
🔹 Sales Trend Analysis
Sales gradually increased throughout the year, with the highest revenue recorded in November and December.
Lower sales were observed during the early months of the year, indicating seasonal demand patterns.
The dashboard helps identify peak sales periods for better inventory and marketing planning.
🔹 Year-wise Performance
Sales showed continuous improvement from 2016 to 2019.
2019 contributed the highest share of revenue (31.92% of total sales), making it the best-performing year.
🔹 Category Performance
Technology emerged as the highest revenue-generating category, contributing 55.55% of total sales.
Office Supplies delivered stable and consistent performance.
Furniture contributed the lowest share, highlighting opportunities for improvement.
🔹 Product Performance
Phones, Chairs, Storage, and Copiers were among the top-performing sub-categories.
Bookcases and Accessories generated comparatively lower sales, indicating the need for strategic pricing or promotional activities.
🔹 Customer Insights
Customers aged 25–54 years contributed the highest revenue.
Younger customers (Under 25) generated the lowest sales, suggesting potential opportunities for targeted marketing campaigns.
🔹 Profitability Analysis
Technology products generated both strong sales and healthy profits.
The dashboard enables comparison of sales, profit, and quantity across different product categories for informed business decisions.
🔹 Interactive Dashboard Features
Dynamic filtering by Year, Category, Sub-Category, and Top N Products.
Enables users to drill down into sales performance and identify trends across multiple business dimensions.
📈 Key Findings
💰 Total Sales: ₹22.97 Lakhs
📊 Total Profit: ₹2.86 Lakhs
📅 Highest Sales Year: 2019
🖥️ Best Performing Category: Technology
📱 Top Selling Products: Phones, Chairs, Storage, Copiers
📚 Lowest Selling Products: Bookcases and Accessories
📈 Peak Sales Months: November & December
👥 Highest Revenue Age Group: 25–54 Years
